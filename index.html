CAFE 
   SIZZLER

The history of "Cafe Sizzling" as a specific restaurant is not well-documented, but the "sizzler" dish originated in 1963 Mumbai when Firoz Erani, inspired by Japanese teppanyaki in California, opened his restaurant called 'The Sizzler'. His son, Shahrookh Erani, carried on the tradition by opening a restaurant named 'Touche' in Pune, which is still operational and considered an original sizzler spot.
